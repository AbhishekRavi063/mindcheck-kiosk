# PRIVACY POLICY
## MindCheck — Mental Wellness App

**Effective Date:** 10th April 2026 | **Last Updated:** 5th May 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

MindCheck is a mental wellness self-reflection application developed by the Translational Neuroscience and Technology Lab (TRANSiT Lab), Department of Cognitive Science, IIT Kanpur, in association with the Center for Mental Health and Wellbeing (CMHW), IIT Kanpur.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the MindCheck application.

By using MindCheck, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the application.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Data You Provide

When you use MindCheck, you may provide the following information:

- **Self-assessment responses:** Answers to validated mental health questionnaires (PHQ-9, GAD-7, PSS, RSES) for self-reflection purposes only.
- **Journal entries:** Personal reflections, text entries, emotions, hashtags, and optional photo attachments.
- **Day log entries:** Daily mood, energy, and stress self-reports through Ecological Momentary Assessment (EMA).
- **Cognitive game performance:** Scores, reaction times, and accuracy from in-app cognitive exercises (Go/No-Go, Attention, Digit Span, Counting).
- **Preferences:** App settings such as dark mode, check-in frequency, and notification preferences.
- **Notification token (PWA only):** If you enable check-in reminders on the MindCheck web app, an anonymous Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) device token is registered with Firebase to deliver push notifications. This token contains no personal or health information.

### 2.2 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect any of the following:

- Your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information.
- Location data, contacts, or device identifiers.
- Data via analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
- Your health data (check-in responses, journal entries, game scores, or day logs) is never transmitted to external servers, cloud services, or third parties. The only data that leaves your device is the anonymous notification token described above, and only if you choose to enable reminders on the web app.

---

## 3. How Your Data Is Stored

All health and wellness data is stored locally on your device. Your check-in responses, journal entries, game scores, and day logs never leave your device.

- **Storage method:** Data is stored using the device's local storage (WebView localStorage) and Android Preferences (Capacitor Preferences).
- **No cloud backup:** Health data is not synced to any cloud service. If you uninstall the app or clear app data, your data will be permanently deleted.
- **User ID:** A randomly generated anonymous identifier (e.g., `user-1710932000000-abc123`) is created on first use. This ID is stored locally and is not linked to your personal identity.
- **Notification token (PWA only):** If you enable reminders on the web app, your anonymous User ID, FCM device token, and notification preferences (frequency and time slot) are stored in Firebase Firestore. No health data is included. You can remove this at any time by disabling reminders in **Profile → Check-in & Reminders**, which deletes the token from Firebase immediately.

---

## 4. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

- Display your self-assessment scores and trends over time.
- Show your journal entries and mood history.
- Track cognitive game performance and improvement.
- Provide personalised self-reflection insights based on your entries.
- Send optional push notification reminders to check in (PWA only, and only if you have enabled reminders).

We do not sell, share, rent, or transfer your health data to any third party for any purpose.

---

## 5. Data Export, Deletion, and Your Control

### 5.1 Export Your Data

You can export all your data at any time from **Profile → Export My Data**. Supported formats include JSON (full data), CSV (spreadsheet), and plain text (readable summary). Exported files include your anonymous User ID for your reference.

### 5.2 Delete Your Data

You can delete your data at any time from **Profile → Clear All Data**. Two options are available:

- **Clear health data only:** Removes check-ins, journals, game scores, and day logs while keeping your preferences and account settings.
- **Full reset:** Deletes everything and restarts the app as if freshly installed.

You can also delete all data by uninstalling the app from your device. Since no health data is stored externally, we cannot recover any deleted data.

To remove your notification token from Firebase (PWA only), disable reminders in **Profile → Check-in & Reminders** before uninstalling.

---

## 6. Data Security

All health data is stored locally on your device. The security of this data depends primarily on the security of your device. For the PWA notification token stored in Firebase, access is restricted to the MindCheck backend functions used solely for notification delivery.

We recommend the following to protect your data:

- Use a screen lock (PIN, pattern, fingerprint, or face unlock) on your device.
- Keep your device's operating system updated.
- Do not share your device with unauthorised individuals if your MindCheck data is sensitive to you.

## 7. Children's Privacy

MindCheck is intended for users aged 18 and above (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are under 18, please use this app only with parental or guardian guidance.

---

## 8. Medical Disclaimer and No Clinical Advice

MindCheck is NOT a diagnostic tool, medical device, or substitute for professional mental health care. The questionnaires used (PHQ-9, GAD-7, PSS, RSES) are validated self-report instruments intended for self-reflection only. Scores and results should not be interpreted as clinical diagnoses.

The app is intended for general wellness and informational purposes only. It is not a substitute for professional diagnosis, treatment, or therapy. Always seek advice from a qualified healthcare provider for any medical or mental health concerns.

If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a qualified mental health professional or use the crisis support resources provided within the app.

## 10. Third-Party Services

MindCheck does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not contain advertisements. External links provided in the Support Resources section are for user convenience only and are subject to those organisations' own privacy policies.

**Firebase (Google) — PWA only:** The MindCheck web app uses Firebase solely for delivering push notifications when reminders are enabled. Firebase receives only an anonymous device token and notification preferences (frequency and time slot) — no health or personal data is ever sent. Firebase's privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy. You can opt out at any time by disabling reminders in Profile.
